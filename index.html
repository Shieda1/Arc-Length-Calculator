What is the arc length formula?
Arc length is the distance between two points along a section of a curve. Determining the length of an irregular arc segment by a pproximating the arc segment as connected line segments is also called curve rectification.
How to calculate arc length formula?
To calculate arc length formula you should use the following formula:
s = r * Φ
where:
s = arc length in radians
r = radius
Φ = central angle in radians
Why use arc length formula?
The arc length formula is used in order to measure the distance along the curved line making up a segment of a circle (arc).
